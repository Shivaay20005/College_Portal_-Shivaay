The Journey of ANDC

Explore the rich legacy, journey, and milestones of Acharya Narendra Dev College (ANDC), a premier institution under the University of Delhi.

Acharya Narendra Dev College (ANDC), a constituent college of the University of Delhi, was established in 1991. Named after the great freedom fighter, educationist, and philosopher Acharya Narendra Dev, the college reflects his ideals of selfless service and inclusive education.

ANDC started its journey with humble beginnings but soon gained a reputation for academic excellence and innovation in teaching. The college was founded with the vision of fostering scientific education and holistic development in students.

The college is located in Govindpuri, Kalkaji, New Delhi, and offers various undergraduate programs, including B.Sc. (Hons) in Biomedical Science, Botany, Chemistry, Computer Science, and many more. ANDC is renowned for its emphasis on research-driven learning and a forward-thinking approach to education.

Through its efforts to offer world-class education, ANDC has built a solid foundation for students to excel not only in academics but also in various extracurricular activities. Over the years, ANDC has grown to be known for its inclusivity and eco-friendly campus initiatives.

Today, Acharya Narendra Dev College stands as one of the most prestigious colleges of the University of Delhi, with a legacy built on dedication, quality education, and continuous progress. As the college continues to innovate, it remains committed to creating an environment where students can nurture their academic pursuits and develop leadership qualities for the future.

Historical Milestones

Acharya Narendra Dev College achieved several milestones since its establishment:

  • In 1991, ANDC was founded with the vision to create an institution that balances both academics and extra-curricular activities.
  • By 2005, the college introduced advanced courses in emerging scientific fields such as Biomedical Science and Electronics.
  • In 2010, ANDC achieved recognition for its efforts in sustainable campus development, launching its green initiatives.
  • As of 2020, ANDC is known for its cutting-edge research programs and collaboration with international universities.

Looking forward, Acharya Narendra Dev College is determined to continue its legacy by embracing technology, fostering innovation, and contributing to the academic and social growth of its students.
