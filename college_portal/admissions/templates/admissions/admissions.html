ðŸ“„ Required Documents for Admission

1. Aadhar Card
A government-issued ID that is essential for verifying your name, date of birth, and residential address. Ensure the Aadhar card is linked to your mobile number for seamless verification.
2. Passport Size Photograph
A recent passport-size photograph with a white background is required. The photo must be in JPG/PNG format and should not be older than six months.
3. Signature
A scanned image of your signature in blue or black ink is required. The file format should be JPG or PNG, with a resolution of at least 300 DPI.
4. Parent/Guardian ID Proof
You are required to submit a government-issued ID proof of your parent or guardian, such as an Aadhar card or PAN card, to confirm their identity. This document is essential for verifying the guardian's authorization, especially if you are a minor or require parental consent for your admission. Ensure the document is valid.
5. Class 10 & 12 Mark Sheets
Your class 10 and 12 mark sheets are necessary for academic verification. These mark sheets will be used to assess your eligibility for the course and your merit based on the marks obtained. Please ensure that the mark sheets are official, original, and attested as per the institutionâ€™s requirements for smooth processing.
6. Caste Certificate (if applicable)
For applicants belonging to SC, ST, OBC, or EWS categories, a valid caste certificate issued by the competent authority is required. The certificate should be recent (issued within the last six months) and needs to be properly attested. This is necessary to avail of the reservation benefits during the admission process and ensure eligibility.
7. Transfer Certificate (TC)
A Transfer Certificate issued by your previous school or institution is mandatory to verify your eligibility for admission. This document confirms that you have successfully completed your previous academic year and are eligible to join a new institution. Ensure that the certificate is attested by your school or college for authenticity.
8. Migration Certificate (if applicable)
If you are transferring from a different educational board or university, you must submit a Migration Certificate. This document is essential for verifying your transition from one institution to another and is required for the smooth processing of your admission. Ensure it is issued by the board or university from which you are migrating.
9. Subjects Studied List
You must submit a list of the subjects you studied during class 12. This list can either be mentioned on your marksheet or provided as a separate document. It is crucial to ensure that the list clearly highlights the subjects studied, as it will be used for subject-specific eligibility verification and to map your academic qualifications to the course you're applying for.
10. Admission Category Proof
If you are applying under a special admission category such as sports, EWS, CW (Children of War Widows), etc., you need to provide relevant proof. This could include a certificate or official document that validates your claim. Such proofs are necessary to secure admission under quota-based or reserved category provisions, as per the institution's policies.
11. Address Proof
A government-issued document that shows your current residential address is required for verification purposes. Accepted documents include Aadhar card, voter ID, utility bills, or bank statements. The address proof is crucial to confirm your permanent or current residence, which will be recorded in the admission records and used for correspondence and other administrative purposes.
12. Blood Group Certificate (Optional)
Although optional, a blood group certificate issued by a recognized medical laboratory is highly recommended. This certificate can be useful for health and emergency purposes during your time at the institution, ensuring that your blood type is on record. It adds an extra layer of safety, particularly if the institution organizes medical check-ups or needs quick access to health-related information.
13. Hostel/Transport Request (if needed)
If you require hostel accommodation or transport facilities provided by the institution, you must submit a request form. This form is necessary to confirm your need for these services and to ensure proper arrangements are made in advance. Submitting this request early will help in better planning and allocation of resources by the institution.
